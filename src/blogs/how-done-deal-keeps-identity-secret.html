Exploring a sale shouldn't mean tipping off competitors, customers or your team. Anonymity is the default on Done Deal — by design, not by request.

The biggest reason founders hesitate to explore a transaction is fear of exposure. We built the platform so that exploring costs you no confidentiality.

Anonymous by default

Your inputs generate an anonymised cue card. Interested investors see the profile — never your name — until you decide to reveal it.

  • You see the investor's identity before they see yours
  • You control if and when your details are shared
  • You can assess market interest without ever going public

Reveal on your terms

When a profile looks right, you grant access. The decision to step out of anonymity is always the founder's — and always reversible until you choose to engage.
