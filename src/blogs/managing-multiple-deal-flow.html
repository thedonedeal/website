Active investors don't lose deals because the opportunities aren't there. They lose them because attention is finite, and a strong pipeline quickly becomes a tangle of half-remembered conversations.

The most common failure mode we see isn't a bad thesis — it's a good thesis spread too thin across too many tabs, threads and spreadsheets. Done Deal is built to collapse that sprawl into one organised pipeline.
