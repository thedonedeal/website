Ritualistic secures $1 million to celebrate Indian heritage in home décor.

A brand with a clear, culturally rooted point of view found the partner it needed to scale — matched through Done Deal.

The deal

Ritualistic raised $1 million from Deep Bajaj, capital that will help the brand grow its mission of celebrating Indian heritage through thoughtfully designed home décor.

Why it matters

Distinctive consumer brands with authentic positioning continue to attract strategic backers — a reminder that a strong identity is itself a moat.
