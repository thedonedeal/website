The CIM is the document that turns interest into intent. Done well, it answers a serious buyer's questions before they're asked.

A Confidential Information Memorandum is the detailed dossier shared with qualified buyers once mutual interest is established. Think of it as the company's case, made in full.

What a strong CIM contains

  • Business overview — what you do and why it works
  • Financials — historicals and a defensible forward view
  • Market and moat — where you sit and why you win
  • The ask — structure, timeline and rationale

CIM vs cue card

A cue card opens the door anonymously; the CIM walks a vetted buyer through the house. On Done Deal the two work in sequence — anonymity first, depth once trust is established.
